A journey of true love

Our story begins in 2004, at James Madison University. We were introduced as we both were to be studying abroad the following semester. Months later, together we took flight and crossed the Atlantic to London, where we uncovered our shared passion for discovering new places and under-going brave adventures.

But our stars did not align until years later, in Washington DC. where, for the past three years, we have grown as true companions. One gorgeous Sunday afternoon in November, Dave set Erin on a treasure hunt. The clues all led to Roosevelt Island where her soon-to-be fiancé stood waiting.

Together they are a delightful twosome. At soireés,at least one of them is guaranteed to own the dance floor.
