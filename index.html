Erin McGill & David DeSandro

November 12, 2011

West Chester, PA

We’re getting married

A journey of true love

Our story begins in 2004, betwixt the Blue Ridge Mountains at James Madison University where the elegant Erin first met the dashing David. They were introduced as both were to be studying abroad the following semester. Months later, together they took flight and crossed the Atlantic to London where they uncovered their shared passion for discovering new places and under-going brave adventures But their stars did not align until years later, in Washington DC

One gorgeous Sunday afternoon in November, Dave set Erin on a treasure hunt. The clues all led to Roosevelt Island where her soon-to-be fiancé stood waiting.

Together they are a delightful twosome. At soireés,at least one of them is guaranteed to own the dance floor.
