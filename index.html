Our Story

Our story begins in 2004, at James Madison University. We were introduced as we both were to be studying abroad the following semester. Months later, together we took flight and crossed the Atlantic to London, where we uncovered our shared passion for discovering new places and under-going brave adventures.

Our stars did not align until years later, in Washington DC. where, for the past three years, we have grown as true companions.

One gorgeous Sunday afternoon in November, Dave sent Erin on a treasure hunt. The clues all led to Roosevelt Island where her soon-to-be fianc√© stood waiting.

Directions

From Points South on I-95 to St. Agnes Church

  • Head North on I-95.
  • Continue on I-95 N towards Philadelphia.
  • Take Exit 8 for Rt. 202 N towards West Chester. Go 13.4 miles.
  • Rt. 202 becomes High St. in West Chester.
  • Turn left on Gay St.
  • Go 3 blocks. Arrive at St. Agnes Church on the right. Parking is around the back.

From Points West on I-76 / PA Turnpike to St. Agnes Church

  • Head East on I-76.
  • Take the PA-100 exit, EXIT 312, toward Downingtown.
  • Merge onto N Pottstown Pike. Go 7.6 miles.
  • Continue on High St for 1 mile.
  • Turn right onto W. Gay St.
  • Go 3 blocks. Arrive at St. Agnes Church on the right. Parking is around the back.

St. Agnes Church to Mendenhall Inn

  • Make a left on to New St.
  • At the next block, turn left on to W Market St. Go 3 blocks.
  • Turn right on to High St.
  • Continue on High St, which becomes Rt 202 S / 322 E, for 6.5 miles.
  • Make right on to Rt. 1 / Baltimore Pike West.
  • Continue directions with Painter's Crossing to Mendenhall Inn, or Painter's Crossing to Hilton Garden Inn Kennett Square.

Painter's Crossing to Mendenhall Inn

  • Head West on Rt 1 / Baltimore Pike. Go 6 miles.
  • Turn slight Left on to Hamorton Cuttoff Road.
  • Then make another left on to Rt 52 South.
  • Go 1.1 mile. Arrive at Mendenhall Inn on the left.

Painter's Crossing to Hilton Garden Inn Kennett Square

  • Head West on Rt 1 / Baltimore Pike. Go 7.8 miles.
  • Arrive at the Hilton Garden Inn on the right, past the Sears and Applebee's.
